Traditional Boat

  1. Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.
  2. Fold the paper in half the other way.
  3. Fold the top corners down and to the centre.
  4. Fold the top layer of paper up along the dotted line.
  5. Fold the bottom layer of paper up and behind the model.
  6. Open up the paper the middle. Push in the sides while pulling the middle forward and back. Flatten everything.
  7. Fold the top flap of paper up along the dotted line then repeat behind.
  8. Pull the paper back out like you did in step 6.
  9. Pull both ends out.

Catamaran

  1. Fold the paper in half and the unfold it.
  2. Fold both sides of the paper in towards the centre.
  3. Fold the top and bottom parts of the paper towards the centre.
  4. Unfold both of these two folds.
  5. Open up the bottom part of the paper along the dotted lines.
  6. Squash Fold the paper down.
  7. Open up the paper at the top just like you did on the bottom and then Squash Fold it flat.
  8. Open up the paper at the top just like you did on the bottom and then Squash Fold it flat.
